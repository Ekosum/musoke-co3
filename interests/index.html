A little about where I'm from

Uganda is a country in East Africa. The country has abundant wildlife, it is home to the endangered mountain gorillas and has nearly 1,061 different bird species. The tourist infrastructure in the country is well developed. Uganda is a perfect vacation spot for those that like to visit a country with a tropical climate. The official language of Uganda is English, but languages such as Swahili and Luganda are also used. There are several tribes in the country all speaking different languages all having different cultures making a visit to every region interesting. This mainly due to different cultures and norm. The local currency is Ugandan Shillings.
